Before You Start

  1. Choose fresh eggs at room temperature for best results.
  2. Prepare a pot of water large enough to fully submerge your eggs.
  3. Fill the pot with water and wait till it boils.
  4. Add a pinch of salt to the water to help prevent cracking.
  5. Gently lower the eggs into the water using a spoon.
  6. Start the timer and wait for it to end!

Cooking Times

  • Soft Boiled: 3 minutes - Runny yolk, soft white
  • Medium Boiled: 5 minutes - Jammy yolk, firm white
  • Hard Boiled: 7 minutes - Fully cooked yolk and white

After cooking, immediately place eggs in ice water to stop the cooking process.

Let's Go!
